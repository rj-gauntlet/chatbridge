ChatBridge
AI chat platform for K-12 education where third-party apps live inside the chat experience. Students play chess, work flashcards, draw on a canvas, and build Spotify playlists without leaving the chat window. Built on Chatbox fork + Express + Supabase + OpenAI GPT-4o-mini.
